About

The Chapter

This platform is run by the Microsoft Student Ambassadors of Saranathan College of Engineering, Department of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science. The Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador (MLSA) program connects students worldwide who are passionate about technology, community building, and helping their peers learn — this is the chapter's official home for daily updates, workshops, and event registration.

Here you'll find announcements on certifications, hands-on sessions on Microsoft technologies, and opportunities to get involved — whether that's attending a workshop, joining a hackathon prep session, or just following along with what the chapter is building.
